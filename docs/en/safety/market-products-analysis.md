## üî¨ What Testing Has Revealed About These Products

### FDA and International Testing Results:
1. **Dosage Variability**: Actual content varies from 0% to 200% of claimed dose
2. **Contamination**: Heavy metals, bacteria, printer ink found
3. **Hidden Ingredients**: Undeclared pharmaceuticals in "natural" products
4. **Wrong Ingredients**: Different drugs than claimed
5. **Dangerous Additives**: Local anesthetics, other prescription drugs

### Specific Findings:
- "Herbal" products containing 3x normal sildenafil dose
- Tablets with no active ingredient at all
- Products containing multiple unlisted drugs
- Contamination with industrial chemicals

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Why These Products Exist

### The Unregulated Market Ecosystem:
1. **Lower costs** attract vulnerable consumers
2. **No prescription required** appeals to those avoiding doctors
3. **Online availability** provides anonymity
4. **Aggressive marketing** targets insecurities
5. **Legal loopholes** in some countries allow production

### The Indian Generic Industry:
- Some legitimate companies making approved generics
- Many unregulated manufacturers making unapproved combinations
- Products made for markets with less regulation
- Often sold online to countries where they're illegal

## üìã Identification Checklist

### How to Identify Potentially Dangerous Products:

#### Visual Inspection:
- [ ] Unusual colors or shapes
- [ ] Poor quality printing
- [ ] No proper packaging
- [ ] Missing pharmaceutical markings
- [ ] Inconsistent tablet appearance

#### Packaging Red Flags:
- [ ] No lot numbers
- [ ] No expiration date
- [ ] Spelling errors
- [ ] No manufacturer address
- [ ] Claims seem exaggerated

#### Source Red Flags:
- [ ] No prescription required
- [ ] Sold via social media
- [ ] Extremely low price
- [ ] Ships from unexpected country
- [ ] No licensed pharmacy involvement

## üö® Emergency Information

### If Someone Has Taken Unknown Products:

#### Immediate Actions:
1. **Note the time** and amount taken
2. **Save the packaging** and any remaining product
3. **Don't induce vomiting** unless directed by poison control
4. **Call poison control** or emergency services
5. **Be honest** with medical providers

#### Emergency Numbers:
- **USA**: 911 or 1-800-222-1222 (Poison Control)
- **UK**: 999 or 111 (NHS)
- **EU**: 112
- **China**: 120
- **India**: 108
