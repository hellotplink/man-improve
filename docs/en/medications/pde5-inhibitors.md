## What are PDE5 Inhibitors?

PDE5 inhibitors are a class of medications used primarily to treat erectile dysfunction (ED). They work by blocking the enzyme phosphodiesterase type 5, which helps maintain erections by increasing blood flow to the penis.

## Available Medications

### 1. Sildenafil (Viagra¬Æ)
- **First approved**: 1998
- **Onset**: 30-60 minutes
- **Duration**: 4-5 hours
- **Food interaction**: High-fat meals delay absorption
- **Common side effects**: Headache, flushing, blue-tinted vision
- **Unique characteristics**: The original ED medication, known for reliable effectiveness

### 2. Tadalafil (Cialis¬Æ)
- **First approved**: 2003
- **Onset**: 30-45 minutes
- **Duration**: Up to 36 hours
- **Food interaction**: Can be taken with or without food
- **Common side effects**: Headache, back pain, muscle aches
- **Unique characteristics**: Longest duration, allowing for more spontaneity

### 3. Vardenafil (Levitra¬Æ)
- **First approved**: 2003
- **Onset**: 30-60 minutes
- **Duration**: 4-5 hours
- **Food interaction**: High-fat meals reduce effectiveness
- **Common side effects**: Headache, flushing, stuffy nose
- **Unique characteristics**: High potency at molecular level

### 4. Avanafil (Stendra¬Æ)
- **First approved**: 2012
- **Onset**: 15-30 minutes
- **Duration**: 6-12 hours
- **Food interaction**: Can be taken with or without food
- **Common side effects**: Headache, flushing, nasal congestion
- **Unique characteristics**: Fastest onset, fewer side effects due to high selectivity

### 5. Udenafil (Zydena¬Æ)
- **First approved**: 2005 (Korea)
- **Onset**: ~60 minutes
- **Duration**: 12-24 hours
- **Food interaction**: Can be taken with or without food
- **Common side effects**: Headache, flushing
- **Unique characteristics**: Popular in Asian markets, good tolerability

## How They Work

1. **Sexual stimulation** triggers release of nitric oxide (NO) in penile tissue
2. NO activates the enzyme guanylate cyclase
3. This increases levels of cyclic GMP (cGMP)
4. cGMP causes smooth muscle relaxation and increased blood flow
5. PDE5 normally breaks down cGMP
6. **PDE5 inhibitors block this breakdown**, maintaining the erection

### Important: These medications DO NOT:
- Create sexual desire
- Work without sexual stimulation
- Increase penis size
- Cure ED permanently

## Choosing the Right Medication

Consider these factors with your doctor:
1. **Lifestyle**: How much spontaneity do you want?
2. **Frequency**: How often do you have sex?
3. **Diet**: Do you often eat high-fat meals?
4. **Side effects**: Which ones can you tolerate?
5. **Other medications**: Check for interactions
6. **Cost**: Generic versions are available for some

## Generic Medications

Generic versions are available for:

Generics contain the same active ingredient and must meet the same quality standards as brand names.

## Proper Usage Guidelines

1. **Start with the lowest effective dose**
2. **Take as directed** (usually 1 hour before activity)
3. **Don't exceed recommended frequency** (usually once daily)
4. **Store properly** (room temperature, away from moisture)
5. **Never share medications** with others
6. **Keep a list** of all medications for your doctor

## When to See a Doctor

Seek medical evaluation if:
- ED symptoms persist or worsen
- You experience concerning side effects
- You have underlying health conditions
- You're taking other medications
- ED is affecting your relationship or mental health
