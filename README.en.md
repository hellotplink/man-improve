## Natural Recovery Methods

We emphasize the importance of **natural recovery approaches**:

### Exercise
- **Cardiovascular**: Running, swimming, cycling
- **Strength Training**: Squats, deadlifts, hip bridges
- **Pelvic Floor**: Kegel exercises for ejaculation control

### Nutrition
- **Key Nutrients**: L-arginine, zinc, vitamin D
- **Recommended Foods**: Nuts, fatty fish, leafy greens
- **Foods to Avoid**: High sugar, high sodium, trans fats

### Lifestyle
- **Quality Sleep**: 7-9 hours for optimal hormone production
- **Stress Management**: Meditation, deep breathing, relaxation
- **Quit Smoking**: Improve vascular function and overall health

[View Complete Natural Recovery Guide](./education/natural-recovery-methods.md)

## Safety Guidelines

### Absolute Contraindications
- **Nitrates Users**: Never combine with PDE5 inhibitors
- **Severe Cardiovascular Disease**: Requires professional risk assessment
- **Severe Liver/Kidney Impairment**: Use is contraindicated

### Emergency Situations
Seek immediate medical attention if:
- Erection lasting more than 4 hours with pain
- Sudden severe vision or hearing loss
- Chest pain, palpitations, or difficulty breathing
- Severe allergic reactions (rash, swelling)

[View Complete Safety Guidelines](./education/safety-guidelines.md)
